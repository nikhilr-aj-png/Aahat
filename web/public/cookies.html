Cookie Policy

Effective Date: June 19, 2026

Aahat uses cookies and local storage technologies solely to sustain and support your app sessions and UI states.

1. Essential Session Cookies

We deploy standard session cookies to verify your login and keep you authenticated during active usage. These cookies are deleted when you sign out of the app.

2. Local Storage Configuration

Aahat stores preferences like theme settings, 2FA status triggers, and notification sounds in your browser's local storage so they remain active between visits without querying servers.

3. Zero Third-Party Tracker cookies

Aahat does NOT utilize advertising cookies, cross-site analytics trackers, or commercial pixels. Your browsing habit and conversation records are never monitored or shared with third parties.
