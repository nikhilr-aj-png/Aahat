Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

At Aahat, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our secure messaging application and related services.

1. Message Security

Aahat protects data in transit using HTTPS and authenticated Supabase access controls. Messages are currently stored by the service and are not end-to-end encrypted. We will update this policy before making any future cryptographic claims.

2. Information We Collect

  • Registration Data: To sign up, you provide your email address and preferred display name. This information is used exclusively to set up your profile and enable contact discovery.
  • Push Tokens: We generate and store anonymized web push notification tokens on your device so that our servers can notify you of incoming calls and messages when the app is closed.
  • Local Cache: To support offline access, message history and contact lists are cached locally on your device using encrypted browser storage.

3. Data Deletion and Portability

You have full ownership of your data. Under GDPR and CCPA regulations, you can export all your account details or request permanent account deletion at any time directly through the app's settings panel. Deleting your account immediately purges your registration, active session keys, and references from our servers.

4. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our service or compliance standards. We encourage you to review this page periodically for updates.
