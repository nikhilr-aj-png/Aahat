Data Deletion Policy & Request

Last Updated: June 19, 2026

Aahat supports the right to be forgotten. Under data privacy laws (such as GDPR and CCPA), you can request full deletion of your personal account data at any time.

WARNING: Deletion is permanent and irreversible. Your private keys, registration, user profile, status updates, and session logs will be completely purged from our signaling servers and database tables.

How to Delete Your Data

1. Inside the Application (Recommended):
Open the app, navigate to Settings > Security & Key logs, and click the Delete Account button at the bottom of the view. This executes database deletion routine instantly.

2. Web Form Request:
If you no longer have access to the Aahat app, you may submit a request using the form below. Our support administrators will process your request within 48 hours after verifying your identity.
